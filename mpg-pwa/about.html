Our Story

From Consulting Firm to Commerce Leader

Founded in 2002 in Princeton Junction, New Jersey, Market Performance Group started with a simple conviction: that CPG manufacturers deserved a partner who could turn insights into in-market action — not just hand them a slide deck.

Over two decades, we've grown to more than 400 strategy and commercialization professionals, building deep expertise across every major retail channel. We've helped some of the fastest-growing brands in health & wellness, food & beverage, and household products define their path forward and then execute on it — together.

In January 2024, MPG was acquired by Sentinel Capital Partners, further accelerating our capabilities and deepening our commitment to driving profitable growth for CPG clients in an increasingly complex retail landscape.
