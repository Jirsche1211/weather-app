Our six practice areas

01

Strategy & Business Consulting

Great execution starts with a clear strategy. MPG's consulting practice brings the analytical rigor and market intelligence CPG manufacturers need to define winning paths in today's complex retail environment.

We work shoulder-to-shoulder with your leadership team to build plans that are grounded in data, informed by deep retailer relationships, and designed to drive profitable, sustainable growth.

  • Annual and long-range business planning
  • Market entry and category expansion strategies
  • Competitive analysis and white space identification
  • Joint business planning with key retail partners
  • Portfolio strategy and brand architecture
  • Private label and national brand management
02

Omnichannel Strategy & Services

Modern retail has no single channel. MPG helps brands build and execute omnichannel strategies that create a seamless experience across brick-and-mortar, e-commerce, and emerging retail media environments.

Our team combines shopper marketing expertise with deep digital shelf capabilities, helping your brand win at the point of purchase — wherever that happens.

  • Shopper marketing strategy and activation
  • Digital shelf optimization and content management
  • Retail media planning and execution (Amazon, Walmart Connect, Roundel)
  • Channel prioritization and investment allocation
  • Unified commerce strategy development
  • Retailer collaboration programs (Target, Walmart, Amazon, Kroger)
03

Business Analytics & Insights

In today's data-rich retail landscape, the brands that win are the ones that turn information into action fastest. MPG's analytics practice transforms raw data into clear business insight — and then connects those insights directly to growth strategies.

From syndicated data to custom shopper research, we provide the analytical foundation your team needs to make faster, better decisions.

  • Category management and space planning
  • Syndicated data analysis (IRI, Nielsen, Circana)
  • Consumer and shopper research
  • Performance dashboards and KPI tracking
  • Pricing and promotion analytics
  • Assortment optimization and new item forecasting
04

Consumer Marketing & Retail Activation

Breakthrough marketing requires both creative vision and operational excellence. MPG's consumer marketing team develops and executes programs that drive awareness, trial, and repeat purchase — both in-store and across digital channels.

We specialize in retail-centric campaigns that connect brand messaging with shopper behavior at the most critical moment: the point of purchase.

  • In-store promotional programs and displays
  • Digital and social shopper marketing campaigns
  • Brand activation and event marketing
  • Loyalty and retention program development
  • Retailer-specific marketing execution
  • Sampling and trial-generation programs
05

Order to Cash / 3PL

Getting product to shelf — efficiently and profitably — is the foundation of any successful retail business. MPG's Order to Cash practice helps manufacturers optimize their supply chain, improve on-shelf availability, and reduce the cost of serving retail customers.

From demand planning to invoice reconciliation, we manage the operational complexity so your team can focus on growth.

  • Supply chain strategy and optimization
  • Demand and replenishment planning
  • Deduction management and invoice reconciliation
  • 3PL vendor selection and management
  • Retailer compliance and EDI management
  • OTIF (on-time, in-full) performance improvement
06

FleXforce®

When you need expert support — fast — FleXforce® delivers. MPG's embedded staffing solution provides short- or long-term, plug-and-play support across every capability area: marketing, sales strategy, analytics, supply chain, and more.

Unlike traditional staffing, FleXforce® professionals are seasoned CPG practitioners who become a seamless extension of your team — delivering day-one impact without the overhead of a full-time hire.

  • Fractional leadership (VP, Director, Manager levels)
  • Category management professionals
  • Sales and account management support
  • Marketing and shopper marketing specialists
  • Dedicated field sales teams
  • Short- and long-term engagement models
